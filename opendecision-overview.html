What Does OpenDecision Do?

A decision support platform that helps teams evaluate and compare software tools.

1

Set Your Priorities

Users rate what matters most: ease of use, security, integrations, reporting, and more.

2

Compare Options

See how different tools stack up against your criteria with visual charts and scores.

3

Get AI Guidance

"Scout" AI assistant answers questions and provides personalized recommendations.

The Problem We Solve

Companies waste months evaluating software. OpenDecision provides a structured framework with AI support to make faster, more confident decisions.
